Our Mission

Caring for Your Parents When Distance Separates You

MedGaurdian was founded with a single purpose: to bridge the gap between NRI children living abroad and elderly parents in India who need consistent, professional healthcare support.

We operate as a full-cycle healthcare supervision partner — monitoring health, coordinating doctors, arranging diagnostics, and delivering clear reports to families worldwide.

Scheduled Caregiver Visits Regular in-home visits by trained, verified healthcare attendants.
Complete Medical Coordination Doctor appointments, diagnostics, and hospital visits — fully managed.
Transparent Family Updates Structured reports delivered directly to NRI families abroad.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Care Services

From daily vitals monitoring to hospital accompaniment, MedGaurdian covers every aspect of your parents' healthcare needs.

Home Health Monitoring

Trained caregivers visit your parents at home to record vital signs and health indicators, keeping you fully informed.

Doctor Appointment Coordination

We schedule, manage, and escort your parents to all medical consultations, ensuring nothing is missed or misunderstood.

Diagnostic Coordination

All blood tests, scans, and lab investigations coordinated and accompanied by our trained caregivers.

Medical Updates to Family

Structured health reports, consultation summaries, and medication updates delivered directly to you, wherever you are.
