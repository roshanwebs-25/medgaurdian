What We Offer

Our Services

Every service MedGaurdian provides is designed to give your parents professional care and you complete peace of mind — from wherever you are in the world.

Service 01

Home Health Monitoring

Our trained healthcare attendants conduct scheduled home visits to monitor your parents' health with clinical precision. Every visit is documented and digitally shared with you in real time.

We record all critical health indicators — blood pressure, blood sugar, pulse rate, oxygen saturation, and temperature — and maintain a comprehensive vitals history so trends are easily visible over time.

What's Included
Blood pressure monitoring
Blood glucose level tracking
Oxygen saturation (SpO2) measurement
Temperature and pulse rate checks
Digital vitals logbook shared with family
Service 02

Doctor Appointment Coordination

Navigating hospital systems can be complex and exhausting — especially for elderly parents alone. MedGaurdian manages the entire process, from booking to escort to follow-up.

Our team schedules consultations at the right hospitals, accompanies your parents to every appointment, ensures medical advice is clearly understood, and handles all follow-up requirements.

What's Included
Hospital appointment scheduling
Physical escort to consultations
Prescription documentation and clarification
Follow-up appointment scheduling
Consultation summary shared with NRI family
Service 03

Diagnostic Coordination

Doctors often recommend a series of tests — blood work, imaging, scans — that require multiple appointments and significant coordination. MedGaurdian manages all of this seamlessly.

Our caregivers accompany your parents to diagnostic centers, ensure every recommended test is completed, and deliver properly documented results back to the care team and to you.

What's Included
Blood test coordination
Laboratory investigation scheduling
Imaging and scan facilitation
Physical accompaniment when needed
Results documentation and family reporting
Service 04

Medical Updates to Family

Distance should never mean uncertainty. MedGaurdian ensures NRI families receive clear, structured, and timely updates about every aspect of their parents' healthcare — directly to them, wherever they are.

From vitals reports to consultation summaries, medication instructions to follow-up plans — you will always know exactly what is happening and what comes next.

What's Included
Vitals monitoring reports after each visit
Doctor consultation summaries
Diagnostic test outcomes
Medication and dosage instructions
Recommended follow-up actions
